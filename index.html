Our project is

Our project leverages computer vision techniques and deep learning models to enhance security and surveiliance systems. In addition to visual images, we used audio and text to data to warn danger to the store manager. This allows for the identification of suspicious activities, rapid response to potential security threats, and efficient milestone for the management of large-scale surveillance operations. Our AI service not only improves the accuracy and reliability of monitoring but also reduces the workload on human operators, making surveillance more effective and proactive.
